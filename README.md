## 🛡️ Project 1: Internal Security Audit & Risk Assessment (Botium Toys)

**Scenario:** Conducted a comprehensive security audit for a rapidly growing e-commerce company to identify vulnerabilities and ensure regulatory compliance.

### 🔍 Key Audit Areas:
* [cite_start]**Asset Management:** Cataloged a full inventory of hardware (desktops, laptops, smartphones), software, and legacy systems requiring manual monitoring. [cite: 37, 41]
* [cite_start]**Risk Assessment:** Identified critical security gaps, resulting in a **Risk Score of 8/10**. [cite: 44]
* [cite_start]**Compliance Evaluation:** Assessed the organization's adherence to **NIST CSF**, **GDPR**, **PCI DSS**, and **SOC2** frameworks. [cite: 31, 32, 66]
* [cite_start]**Technical Findings:** Discovered high-risk issues including lack of encryption for credit card data, absence of IDS, and missing disaster recovery plans. [cite: 51, 52, 55]

### 💡 Strategic Recommendations:
* [cite_start]Implementation of the **Least Privilege** principle and separation of duties. [cite: 89]
* [cite_start]Deployment of **AES-256 encryption** for sensitive data at rest and in transit. [cite: 90]
* [cite_start]Establishing a **Disaster Recovery Plan** and regular backup restoration procedures. [cite: 91]
* [cite_start]Standardization of security policies using the **ISO/IEC 27001** framework. [cite: 96]
